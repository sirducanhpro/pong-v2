Treasure-hunt Game


About this Sketch

This is a Treasure Hunt Game

Directions

  • The objective is to get as many treasures as possible
  • Players must not touch the flying enemies that will lose one life each made contact. The player have a total of 6 lives.
  • The player cannot move pass the black barriers.
  • You can change the directions of the paddle up and dow using either W,S or UP and DOWN arrow key.
  • Have fun playing the game!
